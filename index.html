📘 Unified Energy Interface (UEI)

UEI is a score that reflects how clean, efficient, and sustainable a state's electricity generation is. It's calculated based on the carbon intensity (CI) and the percentage of renewable sources like solar, wind, and hydro.

Formula:
UEI = 100 - (Carbon Intensity / 10) + Renewable %

Higher UEI = Better energy mix. Targeting 80+ ensures a clean energy future 🌍⚡
